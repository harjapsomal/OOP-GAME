The rooms are shown visually on a 3x3 layout. As you move around (using the navigation buttons), the current room is highlighted.
One room is Heaven, another is Hell. Both are locked, and the keys are in other rooms. If you find the room containing the key, you are informed that you have the key. The key is placed in your pocket. Once you try to enter a locked room, it now opens since you have the key in your possession. You WIN the game by arriving in heaven. You LOSE the game by falling into hell. Every time you play, the heaven/hell rooms are RANDOMLY assigned. The game is designed to be played on a laptop only, not mobile devices. Good luck.
